Enter button.

The circle or cross button that has the meaning of [Enter] with the value of SystemParameters.GamePadButtonMeaning is abstracted and notified as the Enter button.

Back 

Back button.

The circle or cross button that has the meaning of [Back] with the value of SystemParameters.GamePadButtonMeaning is abstracted and notified as the Back button. The Android hardware Back button is also considered and notified.
